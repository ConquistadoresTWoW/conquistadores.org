Guild Rules

Guidelines that keep our community strong and respectful

Communication Rules
  1. Treat all members with respect and courtesy
  2. No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior
  3. Keep guild chat appropriate for all ages
  4. Use Discord for raid communication
  5. English is the primary language in guild chat
  6. No spamming or excessive use of caps
Raid Rules
  1. Be online 15 minutes before raid start
  2. Come prepared with consumables and repairs
  3. Maintain 85% attendance rate
  4. Follow raid leader instructions
  5. No rage quitting during progression
  6. Constructive feedback only during breaks
General Conduct
  1. Represent the guild positively in all interactions
  2. Help fellow guild members when possible
  3. No ninja looting or scamming
  4. Inactive for 30+ days without notice = removal
  5. Alt characters must be disclosed to officers
  6. Follow Blizzard's Terms of Service
Consequences
  1. First Offense: Verbal warning
  2. Second Offense: Written warning + temp suspension
  3. Third Offense: Guild removal
  4. Severe Cases: Immediate removal
  5. Appeals can be made to guild leadership
  6. All decisions are final after appeal process
